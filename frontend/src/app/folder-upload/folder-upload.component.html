MLaaS for Researchers

Simplified machine learning model training and deployment tailored for image classification, designed for researchers across domains without requiring expert ML knowledge, enabling quick prototyping and theory validation.

Train your model

Simply upload your images as a compressed folder. Once done, click "Start Training" to begin the training process. Wait until a pop-up message appears, indicating that the training process is finished, so that you can start serving your model.

Test your model

After the training process is complete, you can test your model. To begin serving, click the 'Serve model' button. Then, upload an image and click 'Classify'. The classification label will be displayed to the right of the uploaded image. If you wish to stop serving the model, simply click 'Stop Model'.
