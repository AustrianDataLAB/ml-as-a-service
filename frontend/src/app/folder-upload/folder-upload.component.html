MLaaS for Researchers

Simplified machine learning model training and deployment tailored for image classification, designed for researchers across domains without requiring expert ML knowledge, enabling quick prototyping and theory validation.

Train your model

Simply upload your images as a compressed folder.
Once done, click "Start Training" to begin the training process.

Start training »

Test your model

When the training is done, you can try your model. To start serving press on the Start button, then upload an image and press on Classification. Right of the uploaded image the classification label will be displayed. When you don't want to serve the model anymore, press on Stop model.
